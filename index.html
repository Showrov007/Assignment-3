Panigale V4 R

Ducati Panigale V4 is powered by 1103 cc engine. This Panigale V4 engine generates a power of 218.48 PS @ 13000 rpm and a torque of 123.6 Nm @ 9500 rpm. The claimed mileage of Panigale V4 is 13.1 kmpl. Ducati Panigale V4 gets Disc brakes in the front and rear.

Multistrada V4 Rally

Type: V4 Granturismo; 90-degree V4 w/ counterrotating crankshaft. Displacement: 1158cc. Bore x stroke: 83 x 53.5mm. Compression ratio: 14:1. Maximum power: 170 horsepower @ 10,500 rpm. Maximum torque: 92 ft-lbs @ 8750 rpm. Fueling: 46mm-equivalent elliptical throttle bodies w/ ride-by-wire.

Monster SP

Type: Testastretta 11° L-twin. Displacement: 937cc. Bore x stroke: 94 x 67.5mm. Maximum power: 111 horsepower @ 9250 rpm. Maximum torque: 69 ft-lbs @ 6500 rpm. Fueling: EFI w/ 53mm throttle bodies. Valvetrain: Four desmodromically actuated valves per cylinder.

Scrambler 1100 Tribute Pro

The Scrambler 1100 Tribute Pro wears striking “Giallo Ocra” yellow paint, which was used on the 1972 450 Desmo Mono and 750 Sport. The sides of the fuel tank feature the iconic 1970s-era Ducati logo that was designed by Giugiaro, and the same font is used to spell “Scrambler” on the top of the tank.
